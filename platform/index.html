What the platform focuses on

  • Compute strategy selection (MCU vs module vs smart module)
  • Power tree definition + proven blocks
  • Motor/analog integration patterns
  • Library‑first discipline for reliable assembly
  • Early mechanical constraints (sketch → STEP workflow)

Status: in development. Today: available through our design services — you get the benefits immediately.

Why we’re building it

Real hardware needs real decisions. The platform is about speed with correctness: repeatable building blocks, documented tradeoffs, and fewer re-spins.

  • Reduce rework by capturing constraints early
  • Standardize proven circuits and library quality
  • Make each revision faster than the last
