What we build

What we build (and what you receive)

  • Electronics: schematics and design decisions that are readable, reviewable, and consistent.
  • Simulation + derating: margin‑aware design where it matters (power, drivers, thermal hotspots).
  • PCB layout: disciplined placement/routing with DfM/DfT in mind.
  • Mechanical: enclosure constraints captured early; sketch → STEP; prototype‑print ready.
  • Handoff: a package that supports prototyping, iteration, and OEM/ODM quoting (CM‑ready outputs in your preferred format).

Built by engineers who ship hardware — not slide decks.

We bring together the skills required to get to a prototype that works and a design that can be built again.

  • Power & Battery management: buck/boost/flyback, protection, rail planning, ultra low quiescent
  • Motor control: BLDC/stepper, drivers, current sensing, switching‑noise‑aware layout
  • Analog front ends: filters, amplification, sensor conditioning, audio
  • Embedded architecture: MCU‑first or module‑first, Edge‑AI‑ready, cellular smart modules
  • Mechanical integration: connectors, mounting, assembly strategy, lowest path to enclosure build

Library pillar

Precision libraries make manufacturing predictable.

A high‑quality library reduces rework, placement mistakes, and assembly ambiguity — especially as volume increases.

  • Accurate 3D models aligned to footprints
  • Complete assembly layers (polarity, pin‑1, outlines, refdes rules)
  • Courtyard standards (IPC, aggressive miniature, or your house rules)
  • Footprints for rigid, flex constraints, and process‑specific needs
  • Team‑consistent schematic symbol conventions
