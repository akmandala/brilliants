Can I start with just a sketch?

Yes. A sketch + constraints is enough to start.

Do you do simulation and derating?

Yes — especially for power, drivers, and hot spots where margin matters.

Do you build prototypes?

We deliver prototype‑ready design packages and can support prototype builds via partners (e.g., CircuitHub or JLCPCB) or your preferred manufacturer.

Can you help us find an OEM/ODM partner?

Yes — we help package requirements, answer vendor questions, and support handoff.

Do you manage Altium 365 libraries?

Yes — including standards, 3D, assembly layers, courtyard strategy, and governance.

What files will we get?

Typically: Altium project, fabrication outputs, assembly outputs, BoM, notes, and enclosure STEP (when mechanical is in scope).
